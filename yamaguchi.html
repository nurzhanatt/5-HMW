TOBIO KAGEYAMA

Yamaguchi is a young man of average build. He has short, black hair, which is often depicted as brown or even green, and is messy and sticks out in all directions. Prior to entering high school, Tadashi was teased for his freckles and excessive bonyness. But now he has grown up and no longer worries about it. After joining the Karasuno volleyball team, Yamaguchi wears a black uniform with orange side panels, black knee pads, and white sneakers with dark gray stripes. Outside of matches and training, Tadashi wears a black gakuran, Karasuno High's school uniform.

Yamaguchi is a rather shy person who most often relies on others. However, Tadashi is quite a nice guy, often laughing along with Tsukishima or just being around Kei when he makes fun of or bullies others. Yamaguchi is a hard worker. This is seen when he asks for more training, or when he continues to practice separately from the team after realizing that he is weaker than the rest of Karasuno. After failing in his match against Aobajosai, he becomes more confident thanks to his teammates and Shimada Makoto.During the summer training camp, he is even put in the place of Kei Tsukishima, who is surprised and calls Yamaguchi cool for the first time in his life. He is a hard worker, as shown when he looked for extra training on his own and how he continued to train outside of the team after seeing how weak he was compared to his other teammates. After a fatal loss against Aobajosai, he gained more confidence thanks to his teammates and Makoto Shimada, who helped him practice his jump serve. During the summer camp, he even stood up to Kei Tsukishima, resulting in him being surprised by Yamaguchi's actions and even calling him "cool" for the first time.Tsukishima began to work harder, and after this event, their relationship returned to normal.
