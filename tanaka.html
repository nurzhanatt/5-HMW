TOBIO KAGEYAMA

Tanaka has a shaved head with a bald patch on his forehead, and blue/gray eyes (golden brown in the manga) with a sharp gaze that leads to the misconception that he is a criminal. He has an average height and a muscular body. Often makes strange facial expressions in order to intimidate others, for which Daichi often scolds him. During his first year at Karasuno, Tanaka had short, fluffy hair dyed blonde.

Due to his peculiar appearance, strangers often mistake him for a criminal. He tends to make intimidating faces to try and scare others, which Daichi scolds him for. He has high moral stamina, being driven into a dead end, he is not lost and continues to play steadily. Loud, short-tempered, and easily annoyed, he is no stranger to picking fights. In addition, he is somewhat of a slacker, as evidenced by his less-than-stellar work ethic at school and his usual lateness to practice. Despite all this, he shows concern and support for his teammates and even defends his underclassmen. Tanaka has a strong interest in girls, as evidenced by the numerous posters of female idols hanging in his room. One girl he is particularly interested in is Kiyoko Shimizu. He often approaches her and tries to talk, though his advances are usually ignored, which he claims turns him on. Tanaka usually looks after her along with Nishinoya, especially during the Tokyo training camp and official matches when the other teams show interest in her.
